About

I’m an investor and thinker working on social capitalism: how to redesign our financial systems so that investors, governments, and communities all win when human lives go better.

I work at Better Society Capital and previously at the International Sustainability Standards Board and Oaktree’s Special Situations group. This site collects my notes, essays, and reading lists as I explore the frontier of social finance.
